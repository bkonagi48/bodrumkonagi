The Konak

In the heart of Bodrum, where the warm spirit of the Aegean meets traditional Bodrum architecture.

Bodrum Konağı is a small, completely renovated boutique hotel built around a quiet stone courtyard. With its whitewashed walls, warm wood, and shaded garden, it offers a peaceful retreat in the heart of Bodrum.

We'll be delighted to welcome you and help you live the real life of Bodrum, from peaceful mornings to the warm atmosphere of the Aegean.
